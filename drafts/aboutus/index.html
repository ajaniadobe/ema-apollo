Our Firm

Since our founding in 1990, we have believed that adhering to our values is integral to our success. This has allowed us to build an innovative and collaborative culture that can support great businesses and generate attractive returns for our clients.

Our History

Our Purpose

Our clients rely on our investment acumen to help secure their future. We must never lose our focus and determination to be the best investors and most trusted partners on their behalf.

We strive to be the leading provider of retirement income solutions to institutions, companies, and individuals.

We are the leading provider of capital solutions to companies. Our breadth and scale enable us to deliver capital for even the largest projects.

As One Apollo team, we believe that doing great work and having fun go hand in hand, and we are proud of what we can achieve together.

Clean Sheet Thinking

We start from scratch on every opportunity, seeking to understand the unique risk-return profile without being anchored by convention.

No Walls

We share information freely across teams. Knowledge flows horizontally, enabling better decisions and more creative solutions.

Flat and Informal

Everyone has a voice. We prize substance over title and encourage direct communication at all levels.

Principal Mindset

We invest alongside our clients. Our interests are aligned, and we think like owners in everything we do.

Right Over Easy

We take the harder path when it is the right one. Discipline and rigor define our culture.

Life's Work

We build careers here. Apollo is a place where extraordinary people commit their professional lives.
